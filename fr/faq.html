FAQ

What is the affiliate program?

The affiliate program works like a commission based system, based on sales of services through entertainment products. The Let’s Bamble online merchant offers its services via its affiliate partners, through the Let’s Bamble Partners program, with which he subsequently shares the profit made - generated by player losses - from letsbamble.com. In the particular case of the Let’s Bamble Partners program, the commission –''affiliate''- partner brings his visitors to the letsbamble.com site, and then receives his monthly commission payment generated by new customers he has referred on letsbamble.com.

Why should I register on the affiliate program?

There are many reasons to register in our affiliate program. It is free of charge, offers high revenue share, plenty of products, so why not monetize your traffic with Let’s Bamble Partners today.

Who can register in the affiliate program?

Every person/webmaster operating their own web site(s) while respecting our general terms and conditions can join Let’s Bamble Partners. Each affiliate partner may only register once.

Are there any joining fees to be associated with Let’s Bamble Partners ?

Joining Let’s Bamble Partners is completely free! There are no charges at all.

How do I register to become a member of Let’s Bamble Partners ?

Signing up to Let’s Bamble Partners is easy! Simply click on REGISTER button and fill in the form to become a member of Let’s Bamble Partners.

Can I register more than one website?

Yes, you can. If your application is successful you can register multiple websites in your Let’s Bamble Partners account. You can create individual tracking links for each site so you can track their performances.

Does Let’s Bamble Partners accept all websites?

No we don’t. We have to review each application individually and evaluate whether its nature or content are suitable for Let’s Bamble Partners or not.

Can I become an affiliate even if I don’t have my own website?

Yes, you can. We provide alternative tools that you can use to promote letsbamble.com. Contact our affiliate managers today at to discuss the options available for you!

How long is the cookie duration?

The cookie duration is 30 days.

How often are my statistics being updated?

Your statistics are updated in real-time.

How much can I earn?

There is no limit to the amount of revenue you can earn. The more effort you put in to sending quality traffic, the more you earn.

Can I promote Let’s Bamble Partners and get commissions from referring new affiliates?

Yes, you can refer sub-affiliate and get additional earnings for every customer that your sub-affiliate sends to letsbamble.com.

How are commissions calculated?

Your monthly commission is the percentage of the Net Revenue generated by your referred players. Net Revenue is calculated as total amount less direct costs associated with the same referrals, such as bonuses, loyalty rewards, payment fees and chargebacks.

How do I get the commissions?

Let’s Bamble Partners calculates commissions at the beginning of each month for the previous month's activity and transfers the commission within the first 2 week of each month.

Do negative balances carry over?

We offer negative carry over policy.
